MOBILE PHONE REPEATER
A mobile phone repeater is essentially a localised signal booster. It listens for weak signals and then shouts them out louder. So all phones, 4g routers etc. near the repeater would benefit. As they essentially broadcast, you need to use hardware approved by the regulator. More info from regulator here

WILLOW

Willow is a good choice for donegal. It can grow in crappy soil, grows quickly, and is useful for living fences, creating elements to obscure unwanted things from view to choreograph your view (e.g. hide your building, or other peoples buildings), and provides materials for weaving, and potentilly is a coppicing wood source.

Sheep will eat the willow buds which will reduce growth, so initially it would need protection from sheep. Pollarding would also be an option (pollarding involves cutting at a height rather than at ground level - in this case cutting above a height that sheep can reach)

  • Hanna Van Aelst, sells willow cuttings of a variety of species. Has a youtube channel with a lot of info - https://www.hannavanaelst.com/
  • Really good video. Using willow as a fence, basket source, etc. etc. - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MPR0-06L2ks

Electric
Solar on the roof would make sense, in which case a maximum area of south facing roof would be desired. To achieve this, an asymmetrical roof that slopes all or mostly north to south would be desirable.
Additional solar panels could be added to a lean-to structure along the south wall if they are needed
Battery bank would be an option. Lithium phosphate batteries can be found fairly cheaply (close to 100EUR per Kwh) at https://ru.nkon.nl/ for example, or sodium batteries may become popular and undercut lithium.
See https://www.youtube.com/@WillProwse for details of solar, battery, inverter etc. systems
