Permissions
Android apps require permissions to do most things. Permissions have a protection level (see right).

Permissions with protection level dangerous have to be confirmed at runtime by the user, via a popup

As permissions vary between android versions, it needs to be taken into consideration that a certain permission may or may not exist, or be required, or need to be confirmed by he user

<uses-permission> is the normal tag to use in the manifest when defining system permissions e.g. BLUETOOTH_ADMIN
<permission> is less common, and used for user defined permissions
