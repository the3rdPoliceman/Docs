Hot Water

How does the hot water work?
The apartment has a water tank that automatically heats overnight. That gets you enough water for about 2 showers and some dish washing etc.
What do I do if I need more hot water?
If you find you need more hot water, there's a switch in the electric box in the large 2 door cabinet just outside the kitchen. If you slide the switch down to the bottom position (labelled hot), it'll heat up a full tank of water
And if I forget to turn it off?
There's no need to turn it off. It resets itself after it heats a full tank
