Using the Television with Apple TV

To watch television channels, you need to use the Salt.tv app, via the Apple TV box. You'll need to use both remote controls - the Samsung one for the television and the smaller Apple TV remote control

  • Turn on the television using the power button on the Samsung remote control, and turn on the Apple TV by pressing the home button on the Apple TV remote control
  • Press the source button, located on the top right of the Samsung remote control until it selects HDMI 1
  • You should now see the Apple TV screen
  • Use the Apple TV remote control to select the Salt.tv app, by using the touch surface at the top to navigate (If you don't see the Salt.tv app on screen, you may need to press menu or home)
  • From here you can watch TV using the Apple TV remote control. You can use the menu button, home button and touch surface to navigate
