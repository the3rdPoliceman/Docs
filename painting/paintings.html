TOOLS AND METHOD
Medium: Acrylics on hard board

Tools: Ikea spatula head, thin sticks, linen thread for stitching leather, hairdryer

Technique: Almost all paint was squeezed straight from tube onto board, then spread with the spatula. Towards the end a few colors were mixed on a palette for the house, highlights etc.

Sticks (3mm bamboo from sushi mat) were used to make some thin lines - wasn't a huge fan of that. The narrow edge fo the spatula was better for this.

Thread pulled through paint used to make smaller highlights

COLORS
Sky blue underlayer: ultramarine + Ti white
Sun: cad yellow
Grey Clouds: Ti white + ivory black + hint of ultramarine
Cloud Variation: Ti white for lights, darks with ivory black
Dark area bottom left: ivory black + ultramarine. Various shades of grey/blue made by occasionally smearing with white and mixtures. Cad red here and there to create purple-ish tones
Brown streak: burnt umber + cad red + cad yellow
Red patch on field: cad red
Green tones in field: cad yellow + ultramarine + Ti white
Yellows in field: cad yellow
Peach areas in field: cad yellow + ti white + hint of cad red
Lines: cad yellow + ti white. Some pure ti white
House main color: cad yellow + burnt umber + cad red
House dark color: burnt umber + cad yellow + cad red
Highlights: Some in ti white, few spots ultramarine, one or two spots cad red
