Features
  • Wood is held at either end similarly to a headtock and tailstock in a lathe
  • The piece can be rotated by a stepper with high accuracy
  • Cutting would be done by a router cutter in a router type spindle
  • Sanding and/or drilling attachments could also be added.
  • I guess it's a 2 axis cnc really - although maybe the spindle would need to be able to rotate to cut barrel shaped spindles
