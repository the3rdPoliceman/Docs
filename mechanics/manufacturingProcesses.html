Injection Molding
A really good explanation by engineerguy
An explanation of tonnage needed by guanxin-machinery. Basically, 0.5 - 0.7 tonnes per cm2 of part area

Important features of the process include
  • Heated barrel - for melting the parts
  • Resiprocating screw - to ensure uniform plastic temp
  • Shallow vents in the mold - allow air to escape but not plastic (5-40 micrometers)
  • Ejector pins - necessary as cooled plastic sucks onto the mold
  • Using draft angles - it's very diffictul to remove pieces which have straight parallel sides. A draft angle makes things much easier
Important things when designing parts for injection molding include )
  • draft angle for release (Covered in this video
  • vents for air (maybe 0.05mm) (Covered in this video
  • place for slug (unmelted plastic from nozzle end) to go (Covered in this video
  • ejector pins
