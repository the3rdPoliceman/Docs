Pressfitting Bearings
If using heat to facilitate fitting, bear (haha) the following in mind
  • A heat difference of 80C should be enough to mount a bearing on a shaft
  • Cooling is better than heating in general, so chill one part in the freezer, and heat the other slightly
  • Never heat a bearing above 125C
