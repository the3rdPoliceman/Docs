Salvaging components
Steppers
Old printers tend to contain a lot of these. Older printers that are no longer supported, and were designed for high volume of printing are the most likely to contain high quality steppers. Examples include
  • HP Laserjet II
  • HP Laserjet III
  • HP Laserjet 4
  • Konica Minolta
  • Brother HL-10
  • Brother HL-8
  • Canon FAX L3100
  • Canon FAX L4600
  • Canon FAX L4600e
  • Canon FAX L920
  • Canon LBP-200v
  • Canon LBP-8
  • Canon LBP-RX
  • Canon LBP-SX
  • Canon LBP-TX
DC Motors
Larger industrial size DC motors (usually brushed DC motors) can be found in:
  • tumble dryers
  • treadmill
  • car starters
