Language Learning Strategy

Steps in learning, and how to implement them

  1. Use spaced repetition techniques to ensure you do not lose what you learned
    Anki
  2. Complete an assessment regularly, to test your progress
    Cambridge Institute Placement Test - 60 Questions
    • 26.04.2023 - Points: 27. Level:B1.I Found most questions difficult, as they require verb congugation skills
    Lengalia Placement Test
    • Not yet taken

    If possible, have ChatGPT generate unique tests in order to assess your current level
  3. Familiarize yourself with the language's basics: Begin by learning the alphabet, pronunciation rules, essential grammar, and basic vocabulary. Focus on mastering these elements before moving on to more advanced topics
    • Create a deck for the alphabet, including all letters and the different sounds they make
    • Research intonation rules for language and create a deck for that
    • Super basic vocabulary: Construct decks for
      • pronouns
      • articles
      • numbers
      • conjunctions
      • conjugation of a regular verb
      • the verb "to be", "to have", "to want"
      • question words
      • negation
      • close family members
      • universal foods
      • animals
      • clothes
      • common colors
      • days of the week
    • Super basic grammar: Construct a deck for
      • Sentence structure, using vocabulary learned previously
      • noun and adjective agreement
      • pronoun usage
  4. Expand vocabulary using word frequency
    Based on a word frequency table, construct a series of decks using the words in the list. Break the content into subdecks based on frequency e.g. 0-250, 251-500 etc.
  5. Expand grammar
    Intersperse the vocabulary decks above with basic grammar lessons. Maybe from here - https://studyspanish.com/grammar
  6. Study Frequently: Allocate dedicated study time to
    • vocabulary
    • grammar
    • listening
    • speaking

    Listening - Listen to natives. Use ChatGPT to generate content, and Text to speech to generate sound files
    Speaking - Speak with natives, talk to yourself, or ???

    Talk to Noelia and Julia
  8. Practice writing
    Have written conversations with ChatGPT
  9. Listening & Reading: Watch video content, and read articles in the language
    Use ChatGPT to translate and simplify articles to your current level, based on latest assessment results
