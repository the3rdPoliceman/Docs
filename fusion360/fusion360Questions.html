What is a snapshot?
A captured particular position of an object
Why is there a timeline?
It allows you to see history, and more important allows you to jump back in time and insert changes at a particular point e.g. If you have created 5 copies of a chair and want to change something common to all the chairs you can jump back to before you created the copies and insert the change there
Whats the difference in parametric and direct modelling?
Direct modelling is just creating forms, like in sketchup. Parametric uses parameters for dimensions etc. and constraints
What is the difference in remove and delete?
Delete removes them throughout the whole timeline and is likely to break the history, Remove removes from the current point in history
What is a spline?
A spline is a curve in 3D space, defined by at least 2 poonts e.g. A bezier curve
What is a b-spline?
A smooth curve created by passing through a number of control points
What are nurbs splines?
A Non uniform rational b-spline - nurbs are essentially B-splines in homogeneous coordinates (don't think i want to go further down this rabbit hole)
What are nurbs surfaces?
A surface created from a network of nurbs splines
What is a t-spline?
