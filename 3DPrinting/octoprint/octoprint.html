Octoprint
Basics
  • Sets up a web server on a raspberry pi, so you can connect to it on your network at http://octopi.local/ and control/monitor the printer
  • To get this to work, i followed the basic guides, then dad to update the GCODE scripts settings in Octoprint settings, following this article from the jyers wiki. I "think" this is only necessary for Jyers
  • This doesn't seem to work with every USB to USB micro cable. I had to try a couple
Octoprint Plugins installed
  • Creality Temperature (1.2.4) - mandatory according to a dude on the internet
  • Creality-2x-temperature-reporting-fix (0.0.4) - mandatory according to a dude on the internet
  • OctoEverywhere! (1.6.6) - make the octopi available on the internet. Mine is here
  • Camera Settings (0.3.2) - advanced camera settings
  • Exclude Region (0.3.0) - See something fucked? This lets you stop certain areas printing on the fly
  • M73 Progress - To update the progress bar and time remaining on the printer display during an OctoPrint job
