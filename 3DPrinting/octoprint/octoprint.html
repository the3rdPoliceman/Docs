Basics
  • Sets up a web server on a raspberry pi, so you can connect to it on your network at http://octopi.local/ and control/monitor the printer
  • To get this to work, i followed the basic guides, then had to update the GCODE scripts settings in Octoprint settings, following this article from the jyers wiki. I "think" this is only necessary for Jyers
  • When trying to use the Arducam IMX519 with octopi, it didn't like it. Installed again from scratch following this guide
  • It doesn't seem to work with every USB to USB micro cable. I had to try a couple
  • Also doesn't seem to like running when the SD card is in
Octoprint Plugins installed

  • Creality-2x-temperature-reporting-fix (0.0.4) - mandatory according to a dude on the internet

  • Camera plugin from the Arducam article above (ok, and here)
  • Exclude Region (0.3.0) - See something fucked? This lets you stop certain areas printing on the fly
