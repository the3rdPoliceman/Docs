Printing Tips
Haven't done much experimenting, but these tips came from various internet videos
  • speed - 15mms-1
  • first layer height - not too close or might jam up. 100% height, 150% width, 50% speed
  • retraction - turn off, but include "coast at end" and "wipe nozzle"
  • extrusion multiplier 1.1
  • no direct support
  • avoid structures that will need jumps, as filament oozes a lot
  • "Avoid crossing oulines for moves" should be enabled
Initial print looked like it had some bubbling/roughness. The following tips were suggested
  • temp too high
  • plastic too humid (probably not as new)
